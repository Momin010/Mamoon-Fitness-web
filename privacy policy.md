# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Forge Fitness & RPG ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness tracking and social platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect personal information you provide directly:
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, profile photo
- **Profile Data**: Age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, experience level
- **Health & Fitness Data**: Workout logs, meal entries, nutrition tracking, exercise performance
- **Social Content**: Posts, comments, messages, friend connections
- **Payment Information**: If you purchase coaching services (processed by third-party payment processors)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect certain information:
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent, interaction patterns
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session tokens, preferences, analytics data

### 2.3 AI-Processed Data
When you use AI features:
- **Food Images**: Photos you upload for nutritional analysis
- **Analysis Results**: AI-generated nutritional estimates and recommendations
- **Usage Patterns**: How you interact with AI features

### 2.4 Third-Party Information
We may receive information from:
- **Social Media**: If you connect social accounts (limited profile information)
- **Health Platforms**: If you integrate with fitness trackers or health apps
- **Coaching Services**: Information from coaches you interact with

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### 3.1 Provide and Maintain Services
- Create and manage your account
- Process transactions and provide customer support
- Synchronize data across your devices
- Enable social features and interactions

### 3.2 Personalize Your Experience
- Customize workout and nutrition recommendations
- Tailor content and features to your goals
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Provide relevant coaching and mentorship matches

### 3.3 Improve and Develop Services
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
- Develop new features and services
- Conduct research and analytics
- Test and troubleshoot technical issues

### 3.4 Communicate With You
- Send service-related notifications and updates
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send marketing communications (with your consent)
- Provide security alerts and important account information

### 3.5 Legal and Safety Purposes
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and the rights of others
- Enforce our Terms and Conditions

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process your personal data based on:
- **Consent**: When you opt-in to marketing or specific features
- **Contract**: To provide services you request
- **Legitimate Interests**: To improve services and maintain security
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with applicable laws
- **Vital Interests**: In emergency situations affecting health

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

### 5.1 With Your Consent
- When you explicitly authorize sharing
- With coaches or mentors you choose to work with
- On social features you participate in

### 5.2 Service Providers
We share data with trusted service providers who:
- Host our servers and databases (Supabase)
- Process payments
- Provide analytics and crash reporting
- Send communications
- Provide AI services (Google Gemini)

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by:
- Court orders or legal process
- Law enforcement requests
- Government agencies (with proper legal authority)

### 5.4 Business Transfers
Information may be transferred in connection with:
- Mergers, acquisitions, or sale of assets
- Bankruptcy or restructuring proceedings
- Due diligence for potential transactions

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data
- **Authentication**: Secure login and session management
- **Monitoring**: Regular security assessments and monitoring
- **Backups**: Regular data backups with encryption

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

### 7.1 Account Data
- Retained for the duration of your account
- Deleted within 30 days of account deletion (except as required by law)

### 7.2 Fitness and Health Data
- Retained for active accounts to maintain historical records
- Anonymized for research purposes after account deletion

### 7.3 Social Content
- Retained while your account is active
- May be retained in backups for up to 90 days after deletion

### 7.4 Communication Data
- Support communications retained for 2 years
- Marketing communications retained until you unsubscribe

### 8.3 Restrictions and Objections

- Right to object to certain processing activities

### 8.4 Consent Management

- Right to opt-out of marketing communications
