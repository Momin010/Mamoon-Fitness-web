# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

LockIn Fitness ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness tracking application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect personal information you provide directly:
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, profile photo
- **Profile Data**: Age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, experience level
- **Health & Fitness Data**: Workout logs, meal entries, nutrition tracking, exercise performance
- **Social Content**: Posts, comments, messages, friend connections, mentorship interactions
- **AI Interaction Data**: Food images you upload for analysis, AI-generated nutritional estimates

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect certain information:
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent, interaction patterns, AI scan usage
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session tokens, preferences, analytics data

### 2.3 AI-Processed Data
When you use AI features:
- **Food Images**: Photos you upload for nutritional analysis (processed only, not permanently stored)
- **Analysis Results**: AI-generated nutritional estimates and recommendations
- **Usage Patterns**: How you interact with AI features and scan frequency

### 2.4 Third-Party Information
We receive information from:
- **Google Gemini API**: AI analysis results for food recognition
- **Supabase**: Database hosting and authentication services
- **Ad Networks**: If you view ads, basic device and usage data for ad targeting

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### 3.1 Provide and Maintain Services
- Create and manage your account
- Process AI food analysis requests
- Synchronize data across your devices
- Enable social features and interactions
- Display relevant advertisements

### 3.2 Personalize Your Experience
- Customize workout and nutrition recommendations
- Tailor content and features to your goals
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Provide relevant mentorship matches

### 3.3 AI Features
- Analyze food images to provide nutritional estimates
- Improve AI accuracy and performance
- Track AI usage for service optimization
- Provide nutritional insights and recommendations

### 3.4 Advertising (When Implemented)
- Display relevant fitness and health-related advertisements
- Track ad performance and user engagement
- Limit ad frequency and prevent abuse

### 3.5 Legal and Safety Purposes
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and the rights of others
- Enforce our Terms and Conditions

## 4. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Supabase
- **What we store**: User profiles, workout history, meal logs, social content, messages
- **Location**: Hosted on Supabase infrastructure
- **Security**: Encrypted in transit and at rest

### 4.2 Google Gemini API
- **What we send**: Food images for analysis
- **What we receive**: Nutritional estimates and food identification
- **Storage**: Images are processed but not permanently stored by us

### 4.3 Advertising Networks (Future Implementation)
- **What we share**: Device identifiers, basic usage data, ad engagement metrics
- **Purpose**: Display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance
- **Control**: You can opt-out of personalized ads in settings

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

### 5.1 Account Data
- Retained for the duration of your account
- Deleted within 30 days of account deletion (except as required by law)

### 5.2 Fitness and Health Data
- Retained for active accounts to maintain historical records
- Anonymized for research purposes after account deletion

### 5.3 AI-Processed Data
- Food analysis results retained with your meal logs
- Original images are processed but not permanently stored

### 5.4 Social Content
- Retained while your account is active
- May be retained in backups for up to 90 days after deletion

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

### 6.1 Access and Portability
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to receive a copy of your data in a portable format

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)

### 6.3 Restrictions and Objections
- Right to restrict processing of your data
- Right to object to certain processing activities

### 6.4 Consent Management
- Right to withdraw consent at any time
- Right to opt-out of personalized advertisements

### 6.5 Exercise Your Rights
Contact us at privacy@lockinfitness.app to exercise your rights.

## 7. Children's Privacy

- The App is not intended for children under 13
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly
- Parents can contact us to request deletion of children's data

## 8. AI and Automated Decision-Making

Our AI features:
- Provide nutritional estimates, not medical advice
- Make recommendations based on patterns in your data
- Do not make significant decisions about you without human review
- You can opt-out of AI features in settings

## 9. Advertising

When we implement advertising:
- Ads may be displayed based on your fitness interests and app usage
- We do not sell your personal information to advertisers
- You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings

- **Backups**: Regular data backups with encryption

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
